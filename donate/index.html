Southtowns Care Center will provide free pregnancy verification and confirmation via a laboratory-quality urine test. We are not an abortion clinic and we do not refer for abortions.

We believe that everyone should have access to the services and information we provide. All of our services are free, confidential, and offered regardless of martial status, age, race, or religion. Some services are gender specific. The services provided by SCC are not substitutes for prenatal care or professional counseling.
