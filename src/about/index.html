Southtowns Care Center, Inc. (SCC) exists to be the the hands and feet of Jesus to women considering their pregnancy options. We will minister the love of Christ and speak words of life to the hearts and minds of our community in the Southtowns of Buffalo.

Mission & Vision

  • Offering compassionate guidance from trained peer counselors.

  • Providing free pregnancy tests.

  • Providing accurate and truthful information.

  • Offering hope and healing through abortion recovery groups.

  • Helping individuals consider the sanctity of life and understanding the benefits of sexual abstinence and purity.

  • Being an emotional and spiritual support for women and their families.

  • Existing as a resource for the community and Christian leaders.

  • Operating with the highest standards in order to uphold the name of Jesus Christ and to love others as He first loved us.

  • Adhering to the Apostlesâ€™ Creed as our statement of faith.

Commitment to Care

  • Clients are served with kindness, dignity, and compassion regardless of age, race, income, nationality, religious affiliation, disability, or other personal circumstances.

  • Clients always receive open and honest answers.

  • Client pregnancy tests are distributed and administered in accordance with applicable laws.

  • Client information is held in strict and absolute confidence and is only disclosed as required by law and when necessary to protect the client or others against imminent harm.

  • Clients receive comprehensive information about pregnancy, fetal development, lifestyle choices, and other related matters.

  • We do not offer, recommend, nor refer for abortions or abortifacients, but are committed to offering accurate information about abortion procedures and risks.

  • All of our advertisements and communications accurately and honestly describe our services.

  • All of our staff and volunteers receive proper training to uphold these standards.

Board of Directors

Alice Moeller Executive Director

Alice Moeller graduated in 2007 and has a strong interest in assisting women facing unplanned pregnancies. She has over ten years of experience volunteering for both nonprofit and health organizations, including Joni and Friends, Inc., Brothers of Mercy Nursing and Rehabilitation Center, the Alzheimer's Association of WNY, Baker Victory Services, Avista Adventist Hospital, and the Buffalo Pregnancy Care Center.

Courtney Jelowski Secretary

Courtney Jelowski is a graduate from Erie Community College with an Associates of applied Science in Nursing. She has 8 years experience working as a registered nurse and lactation consultant with a local pediatric office where she has had the opportunity to help new mothers transition into motherhood. Recently Courtney has transitioned into a management role with a local Internal Medicine and Pediatric practice.

Paul Marks Treasurer

Paul Marks graduated with a MA in Education in 2005 from Concordia University in St. Paul, MN, and with a BA in Behavioral Science from Concordia College in Bronxville, NY. He has significant financial background working with Thrivent Financial where he served as a partner for seven years. He also serves on the Board of Directors for Pioneer Camp and Retreat Center in Angola, NY.

Kate Son Director

Over the past ten years she has worked at a pregnancy resource center, volunteered as a mentor in an anti-sexual trafficking organization, led a mural project in inner-city DC, tutored children from low-income or single-parent homes, and worked as a middle school ESOL teacher.
